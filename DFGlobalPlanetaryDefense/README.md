[Deepflame] Global Planetary Defense
=================================
Global Planetary Defense completely overhauls the endgame objective of building the lasers and computer lab, to ensure the vast majority of specializations are pulled into the endresult, no longer requiring that one Electronics player to complete the game by themselves.

Laser
---------------------------------
The Laser now requires a bunch of complex sub-assemblies that require other complex parts, spanning from baking to hunting, composites to electronics.

Computer Lab
---------------------------------
Like the Laser, the Computer Lab itself now requires a bunch of complex sub-assemblies that in turn require other complex parts.

Various other Specializations
---------------------------------
Some of the new materials introduced also made sense to be used in other crafting trees, soda and bone ash are excellent fluxes for glassworks, starch resin with some glycerol makes for a good polymer, etc. You will find a handful of new recipes offering some alternative economic routes for certain resources.
